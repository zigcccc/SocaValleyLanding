Water Activities

From water action deep in the valley to bird's-eye views high above, there are options of activities for everyone in region's best outdoor destination – Soča Valley!

Cycling

Cycling routes for all levels lead through the most beautiful corners in the Julian Alps. Due to terrain's extreme diversity there are appropriate cycling routes for everyone.

Family Trips

From water action deep in the valley to bird's-eye views high above, there are options of activities for everyone in region's best outdoor destination – Soča Valley!

Hiking

Mountain slopes that surround the valley offer some of the best views in the Julian Alps. Mountains and valleys are interlaced with countless marked trails of varying levels of difficulty and pass pristine forests, magnificent waterfalls and other natural beauties.

Skydiving & flights

For all adrenalin junkies and for those that enjoy bird's-eye views characteristics of Soča Valley offer excellent thermal conditions for one of the best paragliding experiences in Europe.
